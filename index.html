About Me

As an artist with a passion for illustration and animation, I bring creativity, skill, and dedication to every project I undertake. With expertise in Photoshop and Illustrator, I excel at crafting visually captivating artworks that breathe life into ideas.

My proficiency in After Effects and Toon Boom allows me to create dynamic and engaging animations, adding movement and personality to my illustrations. I love exploring the endless possibilities of animation and bringing stories to life through motion.

Having worked as a freelancer, I have honed my ability to collaborate effectively with clients and bring their visions to fruition. From initial concept to final product, I am committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations.

Furthermore, my experience working with companies on projects from start to finish has provided valuable insight into the production process. I understand the importance of meeting deadlines, maintaining consistent communication, and working seamlessly with teams to achieve shared goals.

With a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic flair, I am dedicated to creating captivating visual experiences that leave a lasting impression. I am excited to continue pushing boundaries in the world of illustration and animation, and I look forward to new opportunities to showcase my skills and passion.

During my illustrator internship with Risidio from April 2023 to October 2023, I had the opportunity to work on exciting projects that showcased my creativity and dedication to the field of illustration and animation. I illustrated a captivating collection of screenwriters, which found application on websites and as an NFT collection. This project involved multiple concept pieces and the use of tools like Photoshop and AI to bring these screenwriters to life.

As a part of this internship, I also created a series of engaging social media posts to raise brand awareness for the collection. This experience allowed me to develop a strong familiarity with AI art creation tools and techniques, enhancing my skillset.

Additionally, I contributed to the design of an ordinal service website, which will soon feature NFT collections I've created. Some of these collections comprise up to 1000 unique images, showcasing the diversity of my work.

Beyond my individual projects, I actively supported and mentored other interns, offering advice and creative insights to help them improve their work. I am proud to have created a positive and collaborative atmosphere within the team.

I'm grateful for the recognition I received during my internship. As highlighted in a testimonial: "If anyone is looking for a role model for how to get the most out of their internship, you don't need to look any further than Raymond. He created a whole new category in his role in the design team and supported everyone. He's been one of the shining stars of this year - of all the years! Thank you again for all that you've contributed through your work, and I look forward to welcoming you back in the not too distant future."

As I continue to explore the world of illustration and animation, I'm excited about the possibilities and eager to push boundaries. I'm always open to new opportunities to showcase my skills and passion, and I'm dedicated to creating captivating visual experiences that leave a lasting impression.
