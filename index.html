About Me

As an artist with a passion for illustration and animation, I bring creativity, skill, and dedication to every project I undertake. With expertise in Photoshop and Illustrator, I excel at crafting visually captivating artworks that breathe life into ideas.

My proficiency in After Effects and Toon Boom allows me to create dynamic and engaging animations, adding movement and personality to my illustrations. I love exploring the endless possibilities of animation and bringing stories to life through motion.

Having worked as a freelancer, I have honed my ability to collaborate effectively with clients and bring their visions to fruition. From initial concept to final product, I am committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations.

Furthermore, my experience working with companies on projects from start to finish has provided valuable insight into the production process. I understand the importance of meeting deadlines, maintaining consistent communication, and working seamlessly with teams to achieve shared goals.

With a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic flair, I am dedicated to creating captivating visual experiences that leave a lasting impression. I am excited to continue pushing boundaries in the world of illustration and animation, and I look forward to new opportunities to showcase my skills and passion.
